When you changed the speed of twinkle in the last lesson, the color went back to normal didn’t it? Well, you can tell the computer to twinkle a certain color and speed at the same time. Inside the parenthesis, put the speed first, then the color, with a comma in between.

Try twinkle(1, blue) to make it twinkle slowly in blue.

Can you make it twinkle fast in yellow? What other speed and color combinations can you think of? Try them all out, and then go to the next lesson.
